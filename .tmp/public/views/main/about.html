What it's all about

Shred WA was designed to be the one stop weather application to help you shred the great state of Washington. The weather here can make conditions incredible but it's always a good idea to heck in on what the weather is doing. Feel free to pack for the opposite conditions as well though because you never know what can happen out there but we will do our best to help you make the right decisions.

About the Creator

Shred WA was created by Steven Aldous to selfishly help find the best days to go ride. Steven is no stranger to the shred, but he is new to Web Development. This application was built as the culmination of a twelve week program through General Assembly in Seattle.

How it was built

Stack: AngularJS, Sails, MongoDB, Bootstrap

API: Trail.API, OpenWeatherData
